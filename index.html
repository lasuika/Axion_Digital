Frequently Asked Questions

What makes you different from a larger agency? +

Direct access. When you work with me, you work directly with the expert architecting and building your solution. There are no layers of account managers or junior developers. This means faster communication, deeper understanding of your goals, and unparalleled agility.

What does the engagement process look like? +

It starts with a complimentary Discovery Call to understand your challenges. From there, I develop a detailed Project Proposal and Statement of Work (SOW). Once we agree, we move through my four-step methodology: Discovery & Strategy, Design & Prototyping, Agile Development, and Deployment & Support.

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but a typical custom automation project or API integration is often completed within 4 to 8 weeks. I prioritize an agile approach with clear milestones to deliver value quickly and iteratively.

How do you structure your fees? +

I primarily work on a project-based flat fee. After our initial discovery, I provide a detailed Statement of Work (SOW) with a fixed price for the entire project. This ensures complete cost transparency for you, with no surprise hourly charges. For ongoing support, we can discuss a monthly retainer.

How does your U of T background benefit my business? +

My ongoing studies in Computer Science at the University of Toronto ensure I am constantly engaged with the absolute latest in AI, algorithms, and system architecture. This allows me to apply cutting-edge, academically-rigorous solutions to your business problems, moving beyond standard industry practices to deliver a true competitive edge.
