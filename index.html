Conceptual Case Study

To demonstrate my problem-solving approach, here’s a conceptual solution for a high-complexity industry: third-party logistics (3PL) in the Greater Toronto Area.

Logistics & Supply Chain Automation

Unified Logistics Command Center

Analyzed the operations of a high-growth e-commerce company (like COROS) to identify key automation opportunities within their Shopify ecosystem. The goal was to reduce manual workload, eliminate errors, and unlock data-driven marketing.

Key Challenges Identified

  • No central view of incoming orders from multiple client channels.
  • Manual data entry to bridge gaps between e-commerce platforms and shipping carrier systems.
  • High error rates in inventory and shipping, leading to costly client penalties.
  • Inability to provide real-time tracking data to their clients.

Proposed Solution: A Central API Hub

  • Architected a central data-processing application that connects to each client's store via API.
  • Built a unified "order firehose" to normalize data from all sources into a single, consistent format.
  • Developed custom integrations with carrier APIs to automate shipping label generation and cost calculation.
  • Created a client-facing dashboard providing real-time visibility into inventory levels, order status, and shipping analytics.
